Add a little Sparkle to your Special Day

Large Letter Company Wiltshire is a small family run business that specialise in the hire of large light up letters.

Features

Battery Powered

Powered by battery, we remove the need for any cables or leads. Allowing the option for either interior or exterior display.

Free Set Up

All our products include delivery, set up and collection (subject to location). However, please note a delivery surcharge may apply.

Hand Crafted

Hand crafted at our workshop based in Dilton Marsh. Our light up Letters and Numbers really add a little extra Sparkle to any event!
